Who we are
We were founded by Mrs. Kimiko in the memory of her late brother. Our main objective has always been to provide quality education. Currently, we offer GCE A Level, a prestigious programme of the Cambridge University, UK and Liberal Arts and Sciences undergraduate programme. We provide a friendly competitive environment for our student to flourish and develop their talents and have a bright future.

Message from our Principal

Kenji University has always aimed to help students achieve their goals in academics as well as in extra-curricular activities. We believe in hard work and expect from our students as well. We are open to suggestions from our students and are always open to their questions. The teachers here are highly skilled and are at the top in their respective fields. I would like to say to our students that their future is in safe hands.
